Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI agent solutions.

How long does it take to deploy an AI agent?

Our standard deployment timeline is 2-4 weeks, depending on the complexity of the implementation and the level of customization required. Simple deployments can be completed in as little as one week, while more complex enterprise solutions may take up to 6 weeks.

Can AI agents integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, our AI agents are designed to seamlessly integrate with your existing technology stack. We support integration with CRM systems, ERP platforms, customer support tools, and custom databases through standard APIs. Our team will work with you to ensure smooth data flow between systems.

How do you ensure data security and privacy?

We maintain strict data security protocols, including end-to-end encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and regular security audits. All solutions are designed to be compliant with GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, and other relevant regulations. We can also deploy in private cloud environments for additional security requirements.

What kind of support do you provide after deployment?

All plans include ongoing support and maintenance. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include designated support contacts, regular performance reviews, and continuous optimization. We also offer training for your team to maximize the value of your AI agent implementation.

Can AI agents learn and improve over time?

Yes, our AI agents utilize continuous learning systems that improve through interactions and feedback. They adapt to your specific business context, learning from successful interactions and refining responses based on user feedback and changing requirements.
